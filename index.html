About Me

I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer based in Guadalajara, JAL. My journey into software development began as a self-taught enthusiast, driven by a passion for creating impactful solutions. Over the years, I have honed my skills in both front-end and back-end development, allowing me to design and build seamless web experiences.

One of my proudest achievements is launching an award-winning platform in Haiti that empowers farmers to sell their products online. This platform not only bridges the gap between farmers and consumers but also fosters economic growth and sustainability in the region. The success of this project is a testament to my dedication and love for software development.

I am always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate with others to bring innovative ideas to life. Let's create something amazing together!
